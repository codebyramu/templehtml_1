Welcome to Bhaskararajapuram

A village, quaint yet serene, on the banks of River Cauvery...

Where the Kaveri, Arasalar, and Vennaru rivers meet in sacred confluence.

Home to the Samadhi of Shri Bhaskararaya, the saint-scholar of Srividya.

Where the Lalita Sahasranama Bhashya was completed by a devoted sage.

Guardian of three ancient temples — Bhaskareswarar, Kothanda Ramaswamy, and Vishnu Durgai.

A living legacy of devotion, carried forward by the Srinivas Rama Trust.

Once the home of an 18th-century master of Tantra, Srividya, and Vedanta.

Named in honor of the foremost commentator on the Goddess Lalita's thousand names.

A spiritual epicenter for Srividya seekers across generations.

Where ancient rivers and ancient wisdom flow as one.

Bhaskararajapuram is a village on the banks of the River Cauvery, near Kumbakonam, located beautifully at the confluence (Sangamam) of the Kaveri, Arasalar, and Vennaru rivers. It stands as a testament to deep spiritual heritage and enduring devotion.

The Srinivas Rama Trust plays a crucial role in the maintenance of the village's sacred spaces and the organization of annual celebrations, keeping the rich traditions alive for future generations.
